**4. PATRON BONDS — The Power Shortcut**

Fallen spiritual beings (Patrons — Entity #7) offer power boosts through
the System framework. They present themselves as benefactors, mentors, gods.

**How it works:**

- At certain level thresholds, the
  System presents **Patron Offers** on the blue screen. "A powerful
  being has taken interest in your potential. Accept their patronage?"
- Accepting gives immediate power:
  bonus skills outside your class affinity, stat boosts, accelerated rank
  progression, rare abilities.
- **Hidden cost:** Each Patron bond deepens System
  integration. The Patron gets hooks into your decision-making. Your AI
  starts nudging harder. Your class starts _evolving_ in directions you
  didn't choose. This feeds directly into Counterfeit Evolution (Entity #15)
  — the incremental replacement of what's human with something loyal to the
  System.
- The deeper the bond, the harder
  it is to break. Early bonds are loose — a perk, a bonus. Late bonds are
  chains.

lines running upstream. The Patron's signature woven through the warrior's stat
sheet like malware in a system process. The warrior feels powerful. Nate sees a
puppet with better production values.

**Why this matters for Ch5:** Nobody has Patron bonds yet in the Tutorial — too early. But the _architecture_
for it should be visible. Nate's gamer lens might notice: "The System is
already showing class synergies. Tank and healer pairing. DPS windows. It's not
