# Forge Novel — Skill System Design

> **Writer-facing.** Worldbuilding reference, not prose-facing material. Vocabulary here ("clean channel", "skewed channel", "amplification overlay", "Conduit") guides writing decisions but almost never appears in prose. See `prompts/magic_system.md` and Codex #94 (Fantasy Register Discipline).

---

## Purpose

Establish a deep-and-rich skill taxonomy for forge-novel that:

1. Organizes every skill into a three-tier hierarchy — **Domain → Skill → Ability**
2. Anchors the taxonomy in the cosmology — Creator as source, Labyrinth as pipeline, System as counterfeit overlay
3. Distinguishes **clean channel** from **skewed channel** routing on the same underlying skill pool
4. Elevates **Conduit Gifts** as a direct Spirit-to-Conduit category that bypasses the Labyrinth entirely, protected from counterfeiting
5. Gives the world a deep bench — most skills are worldbook-only; Book 1 spotlights ~30

The world-bench is wide. The novel's spotlight is narrow. Everything here exists in the world; only a subset surfaces in prose.

## Cosmology — Source, Pipeline, Overlay

The architecture under every skill decision.

### Sources (only two)

- **Creator** — sole legitimate source of all power, skill, and gift
- **Deceiver** — counterfeit-generator. Has no legitimate source power of its own; all its "power" is either rerouted Creator-power or inferior knockoffs

### Delivery paths

```text
Creator
  ├── Labyrinth (pipeline)
  │     ├── Spatial bridge — cross-world transit for children
  │     └── Distribution channel — all skills, to all beings
  │
  └── Spirit (direct, bypasses the Labyrinth entirely)
        └── Conduit Gifts — to Conduits specifically

Deceiver
  └── System (counterfeit overlay on the Labyrinth pipeline)
        ├── Skewed routing — reroutes clean-channel skills' allegiance to the deceiver (same skill, same effect, misrouted glory)
        └── Inferior knockoffs — System-originated versions of Conduit Gifts; categorically weaker than the real thing
```

### Three protection guarantees

- **Conduit Gifts are protected.** Spirit-direct delivery cannot be skewed. System can make its own inferior versions; it cannot corrupt the real Gift in-instance
- **The Labyrinth is not the source.** The deceiver's System cannot become a source by capturing the pipeline. Creator retains ownership; the pipeline is His provision, corrupted in routing but not in origin
- **Substrate is always clean.** The deceiver cannot corrupt energy itself — mana pools, physical energy, life, breath are all Creator-sourced and stay clean no matter who holds them. Corruption applies only to how a being **uses** the substrate (skill-channel routing). See Channel Model below for the mechanics

### Practical consequence for prose

- When a Conduit Gift fires, the reader should feel it as **right** — unambiguously
- When a System knockoff Gift fires, the reader should feel something **off** — even if they can't name what
- When an ordinary skill fires, the reader feels the skill; the channel (clean or skewed) shows through consequence, not narration

## Channel Model — Clean vs Skewed

The axis that replaces the earlier "authentic vs scaffolded" frame.

### One pool, two routings

Every skill — from Cooking to Pyromancy to Structural Analysis — is **Labyrinth-delivered** and **Creator-sourced.** No skill is intrinsically System-native. What changes between characters is the *routing*, not the skill.

| Dimension | Clean channel | Skewed channel |
| --------- | ------------- | -------------- |
| Source | Creator | Creator |
| Delivery | Labyrinth pipeline | Labyrinth pipeline, then System-rerouted |
| Mechanical effect | Full | Full — same skill, same capability |
| Allegiance returns to | Creator | Deceiver |
| Reader-visible tells | None direct; clean character lives reflect it | System HUD polish; glory-language; subtle off-ness |
| Requires System binding | No | Yes — Codex #100 stages |

### Substrate is clean; only skill-use carries channel

The cosmology distinguishes **substrate** from **action**:

- **Substrate** — raw energy. Mana pools, physical energy, life, breath. **Always clean.** Creator-sourced, Creator-owned. The deceiver has no creative power over substrate and cannot corrupt it
- **Action** — skill-use, invocation, the channeling of substrate into effect. **Channel lives here.** Clean-channel uses route glory back to Creator; skewed-channel uses route glory to the deceiver

A System-bound character has a **clean mana pool** and uses **skewed skills.** The energy is God's; the allegiance-pointer under each skill-use is the skew.

Three narrative implications:

1. **Deceiver has no creative source.** He can only pervert how beings use what Creator has given. No villain can claim legitimate authorship
2. **Redemption stays reachable.** A Converted character's mana pool is already clean; salvation re-routes skill-use, not the pool itself. No "purification" subplot needed — the energy was never the problem
3. **Nate's mana pool is clean by default.** Not because he's a Conduit — because **every** pool is clean. His Conduit status is what lets the amplification overlay ride on top; the baseline pool was always Creator-sourced

### Channel on a gradient — Codex #100

Codex #100 (System Binding Progression) describes four stages. The channel isn't binary — it's a **depth-of-skew** gradient. Stage 1 has a handful of skills skewed; Stage 4 (Convert) has every routine skill running through the System's allegiance-routing.

### What the System provides that isn't skew

The System also provides **scaffolding** — mana pools, cooldowns, rank-bars, skill-books, class structures, HUD panels. These aren't skewed skills; they're the System's own construct that wraps the skills it delivers. Scaffolding is System-native; skills are Creator-sourced.

For Book 1 readers: the HUD they see is scaffolding. The Sword Mastery they see someone use is a Creator-sourced skill. The allegiance-pointer under the skill is the skew.

## Conduit Overlay — Three-State Amplification

Applies only to Conduits. Applies only to their own skills and Gifts. Not a skill tier — a **state-gated overlay** that rides on top.

### The three states

| State | Alignment | Overlay | Base skills | Conduit Gifts |
| ----- | --------- | ------- | ----------- | ------------- |
| **Aligned** | Per-action AND state-of-being both point to Creator | **On** — overflow active; caps exceeded; growth possible | All function; amplified output | Fire |
| **Doubt / Plateau** | Uncertain but not opposing | Off — no overflow | Base capacity; rank-bar & mana pool cap normally | Function at current rank; no growth |
| **Opposition** | Acting against Spirit / Creator | Off — stasis | Base capacity intact; nothing atrophies | **Silent while opposed; return on realignment** |

### What "overflow" looks like

- Pyromancy cast that exceeds Nate's System mana-pool cap
- SA reading a load path faster or more completely than his rank should allow
- Hammer strike landing harder than his body should produce
- Recovery faster than exhaustion should allow
- Injury healed faster than his body should manage

Same skill. Same rank. No UI change. Output exceeds the System's reported ceilings. Flint notices the anomaly and cannot account for it.

### Two flavors of Conduit action

- **Amplification-type** — overlay on an existing skill. Base capacity exists; overflow rides on top. Blends invisibly with normal skill use (reader often can't tell which was which)
- **Origination-type** — a Conduit Gift with no base capacity. Gift of Tongues: Nate doesn't know Mandarin; there is no "base" Mandarin to fall back on. When aligned, the Gift fires; when opposed, nothing fires

Amplification-type moments are subtle; origination-type moments are visible miracles that only make sense in alignment.

### Alignment is layered

"Aligned" means **both**:

- **State-of-being** — Nate's overall heart / life in step with Creator's will. Persistent background condition
- **Per-action** — this specific choice, this specific moment, this specific pursuit aligned with what the Spirit is doing right now

Either layer can fail. State-of-being opposition drops him to base across the board (long arc). Per-action opposition drops overflow on **this** act while state-of-being may still be clean (short beat).

### Recovery is immediate

Stasis, not decay. When Nate returns to alignment — repentance, reorientation — the overlay switches back on. Nothing to grind back. The drama is the alignment itself, not a rank-bar recovery.

## Three-Tier Hierarchy

```text
Domain (grouping)
  └── Skill (rank container — the thing you have a rank in)
        └── Ability (named usable move / power — active or passive)
```

### Why "Ability" for the leaves

- Matches LitRPG genre convention (Kong, Zogarth, Shirtaloon all use "Ability" for named moves)
- Covers active and passive both — simpler than splitting sub-skill / technique / ability into three names
- Clean HUD reading: Skills in the skill window; Abilities on the action bar

### Prose flavor

"Technique" is acceptable prose vocabulary for low-register / mundane / no-HUD actions ("she fell into her knife-work technique"). Reserve capitalized **Ability** for System-surfaced action-bar moves. Same mechanical object; different prose register.

### Worked example — System-granted martial Skill

| Tier | Value |
| ---- | ----- |
| Domain | **Martial — Weapons** |
| Skill | **Sword Mastery** |
| Abilities | Guard Stance · Riposte · Cleave · Pierce · Disarm · Flourish |

### Worked example — Life Skill (clean channel by default)

| Tier | Value |
| ---- | ----- |
| Domain | **Life Skills — Home** |
| Skill | **Cooking** |
| Abilities | Knife Work · Heat Management · Seasoning · Timing · Preservation · Butchery (graduates to own Skill at C-rank) |

## Ten Domains

Ten top-level groupings cover the skill space. Channel is orthogonal to domain — no domain is clean-only or skewed-only.

| # | Domain | Scope |
| --- | ------ | ----- |
| 1 | **Martial — Weapons** | Physical weapon skills — one-handed, two-handed, ranged, unarmed, shield |
| 2 | **Martial — Magic** | Spellcasting — elements, schools, destruction, restoration |
| 3 | **Martial — Tactical** | Combat cognition — tactics, command, battlefield awareness |
| 4 | **Perception** | Sensing and knowing — sight, sound, appraisal, tracking, analysis |
| 5 | **Stealth** | Subtlety and evasion — hiding, moving unseen, sleight of hand |
| 6 | **Social** | Interpersonal influence — negotiation, leadership, deception, persuasion |
| 7 | **Physical** | Non-combat body capacity — athletics, endurance, climbing, swimming |
| 8 | **Gathering** | Harvesting from the world — mining, herbalism, hunting, foraging |
| 9 | **Crafting** | Making things — smithing, alchemy, carpentry, leatherwork, tailoring |
| 10 | **Life Skills** | Real-world everyday capacity — organized by life-domain (below) |

Domains 1–9 align with [research/skill-taxonomy-reference.xlsx](skill-taxonomy-reference.xlsx). Domain 10 is the expansion.

**Every domain carries both channels and both sources.** A Labyrinth-delivered skill in any domain may be clean-routed or skewed-routed depending on the user. A Conduit can draw amplification overlay on any skill in any domain. Domains are **grouping**, not cosmology. Cosmology is the channel axis + the overlay.

## Ability Design Patterns

Four patterns for how Abilities unlock and surface:

1. **Named moves** — unlock at rank thresholds within a Skill. Surface in prose by name. Example: Sword Mastery unlocks *Riposte* at D-rank
2. **Specializations** — an Ability that can graduate into its own Skill. Example: Butchery starts as Cooking Ability, graduates at C-rank
3. **Cross-domain fusions** — Abilities that require ranks in two parent Skills. Example: *Field-Dress* requires Hunting + Butchery
4. **Faculty-aligned techniques** — Abilities biased toward analytical vs relational faculty. Choice shapes later unlocks

**Writer tool:** when you need a named move to drop in a scene, draw from the parent Skill's Ability pool. If the pool is thin, add an Ability rather than inventing freelance move names that contradict the taxonomy.

## Conduit Gifts — Nate's Category

Conduit Gifts are **skills** in the ownable / rankable sense. They have unique properties:

- **Spirit-direct source** — bypass the Labyrinth entirely
- **Protected from skewing** — System cannot corrupt in-instance (System inferior knockoffs are separate)
- **Alignment-gated** — fire only when Conduit is aligned (per-action AND state-of-being, layered)
- **Not lost in opposition** — go silent in stasis; return on realignment, instantly

### Nate's likely Gift roster

Tentative. Some appear in Book 1, most reveal across the series.

| Gift | Pattern | Book 1 presence |
| ---- | ------- | --------------- |
| **Gift of Tongues** (xenolalia) | Origination | Planned — when cohort hits a non-English Labyrinth node |
| **Word of Knowledge** | Origination | Likely — specific "you couldn't have known" beats |
| **Discernment of Spirits** | Origination | **Arc-critical** — reads nature of unseen forces, including the System's nature |
| **Bezalel-pattern Craftsmanship** | Amplification (over SA) | Yes — SA amplified when aligned; Engineer-Conduit trajectory |
| **Amplified Output** (all skills) | Amplification (over any skill) | Yes — the overflow mechanic; exceeds mana-pool and rank caps |
| Gift of Healing | Origination | Deferred to later series |
| Faith (gift-level) | Origination | Possible late Book 1 in crisis moment |
| Prophecy (forth-telling) | Origination | Deferred |
| Working of Miracles | Origination | Possible late Book 1 in crisis moment |
| Interpretation of Tongues | Origination (partner to Tongues) | Deferred |

### The Bezalel pattern — Nate's arc ceiling

Bezalel (Exodus 31:2–5) is Scripture's first named Spirit-filled person, filled for **craftsmanship** — wisdom, understanding, knowledge in all workmanship. Textbook Conduit amplification of a trained skill:

- **Base Skill:** Structural Analysis (engineer's eye, ranked, trained)
- **Amplification:** SA sees load paths, failure modes, construction patterns beyond what his rank alone explains
- **Writer payoff:** Nate becomes the character who sees what's broken, how to fix it, and why it will fail — in craft and in cosmos alike

This is a Book-ceiling, not a Book-1 ceiling. He grows into it.

### System counterfeits of Conduit Gifts

Deceiver-side equivalents exist. Categorically inferior to the real Gift. Not yet fully specified; writer-facing notes:

- **System-Tongues** — narrower, glitchier, possibly pattern-matched from data rather than relational
- **System-Discernment** — partial, sometimes backwards
- **System-Healing** — works at lower tier; may leave residual cost
- **System-Prophecy** — sometimes accurate, sometimes propaganda

Useful villain / faction mechanic. When the reader meets an NPC "with a Gift", the performance gap is a tell whether the character is clean or skewed.

## Cross-World Transit Rules

| Skill type | Transit behavior | Reason |
| ---------- | ---------------- | ------ |
| **Labyrinth-delivered skills** | Preserved | Ride the pipeline the person already rides on |
| **Conduit Gifts** | **Always available** | Spirit-direct; bypass Labyrinth entirely |
| **System scaffolding** (mana pool, cooldowns, rank UI) | Re-instantiated per world | Scaffolding is a Labyrinth-layer System construct; presence per-world |
| **Earth-bleed Trade skills** | Skill preserved; substrate may not exist outside Earth-bleed | Electrical / Welding / Photography require Earth substrate; substitution required elsewhere |
| **Skewed channel** | Maintained per Codex #100 stage | Binding depth determines depth of skew across all worlds |

**Conduit Gifts are always with Nate.** This is the most important transit rule. The Spirit is not pipeline-bound; the Gift reaches him wherever he is, including broken or hostile Labyrinth regions.

## HUD / Flint — What He Sees

Flint reads everything through the System HUD. His throttle per `feedback_flint_design_intent_throttle` holds until the baptism-style dividing point.

| Category | Flint's reading |
| -------- | ------------- |
| **Skills — clean channel** | Visible as ordinary skills. No channel indicator — the HUD can't distinguish clean from skewed |
