*Status: Spine locked — drafting pending*
*Date: 2026-06-01*
*Authors: David (steer) + Claude (draft)*

---

## What this is

A re-conception of **Chapter 2 — Integration**. The current draft
(`drafts/ch02-integration-draft01.md`) renders the Integration as a passive
event: Nate is a "specimen on a slide" with "no buttons, no console," and the
chapter's engine is that he receives *nothing* — eight billion get the full
release, he gets the loading screen that won't load.

This record replaces that with an **interactive** Integration: a build/state/
theme menu Nate actively walks, where his choices and a System counterfeit
collide and crash. The strong wake-up cascade and two-blue-arcs landing are
**preserved and re-caused**, not discarded.

No canon files have been edited. This document captures the agreed design and
lists the blast radius for David's approval before anything propagates.

---

## The core idea

The Integration menu is **the real, underlying Labyrinth integration** — genuine
divine architecture — **with the System's counterfeit hooks spliced into it.**
Most doors are true. A few are Morningstar (MSG) lies wearing the same paint.

This is the locked cosmology finally dramatized on the page: *the Labyrinth is
the pipeline, not the source; the System is the corrupted fork; the counterfeit
sells labels over true names; the substrate is always clean.* The scene should
read as **mostly awe-inspiring and true,** with wrongness only at the splice
points — the counterfeit is recognizable by its glossiness, not by being the
whole pitch.

Nate's discernment (the Spirit, rendered as intuition) keeps steering him to the
true doors. His **consistency on the clean path is the cause of the crash** — a
man who keeps choosing true is funneled to a gate where every door is a lie.
His integrity, not an accident, breaks his Integration. That is the whole book
in one mechanic.

## The agreed spine

### The pitch

1. **Integration goes interactive.** The passive scan is gone. Nate gets the
   same build/state/theme menu everyone gets.
2. **Real Labyrinth integration, hooks spliced in.** Mostly genuine divine
   architecture; a few MSG lies in the same paint, recognizable by their gloss.
3. **Three tiers of door:** clean Labyrinth options / see-through hooks (look
   better, can be discerned and leaned away from) / outright lies disguised as
   good choices.
4. **Nate walks the clean path by intuition.** Pure somatic pull — he leans
   away because something *felt off*, never because he "caught the lie." He is
   human enough to take **one or two shiny hooked doors anyway.**

### The turn

1. **Adaptive funnel.** Because Nate keeps dodging hooks, the System *adapts and
   tightens* — doors narrow and sweeten until the final gate has **no clean
   option.** He is flagged during the menu; he is on the System's radar from
   minute one. Narrator stays at AI-process register (re-weighting, narrowing),
   never transcendent intent.
2. **The clean-path crash.** At the final gate every option is a disguised lie —
   neither Nate nor the reader knows. Having arrived near-pure, his integration
   has nothing the forced lie can anchor to. The lie cannot seat. Collision.
   The Integration craters on this major false choice.

### The grace

1. **The gap = the Spirit's rewrite.** During the short unconscious passage
   before he wakes, the Spirit does His major rewrites: the **mostly-wiped** core
   is restored to a minimal protected core; the one or two hooks he took are
   **stripped, and the items handed back pure** (sanctification — the same move
   the Spirit ran on Flint's leash); the System's caps are removed.
2. **Limitless = unbounded mastery depth.** No aptitude ceiling — mastery
   *earned, never gated.* This is an **outcome of the Spirit restore** (the
   absence of the System's caps), not a flashy power and not earned in the menu.
   It is the Bezalel pattern and a work-ethic theology: the counterfeit sells
   shortcuts; the real thing sells unlimited earned depth. Slow burn —
   invisible early, decisive by mid-series. Complements his Structural Analysis;
   **depth is the spine, with no-ceiling (uncapped growth) and no-class-lock
   (breadth) available to stack later.**
3. **Memory = quiet grace, no shadow.** Wake-up devastation lands at full force —
   he believes it is all gone. Memories then **resurface over time as the clean
   substrate heals.** No System copies, no leverage thread, no catch. Pure,
   unearned grace, shown by memories quietly coming home.

### The landing

1. **Wake-up = the existing cascade.** The HUD-blooms-then-collapses sequence →
    two blue arcs → **BOOT SEQUENCE PENDING**, kept intact and re-cast as the
    *visible aftermath* of the gap-rewrite. *HUD reads less, effect is more.*
2. **He walks away** near-blank but pure-cored, hooks cleaned, no mastery
    ceiling, memories pending return. **Underdog now, master later — and he had
    to earn every inch.**

## The concrete menu — 4-station walk (locked 2026-06-01)

**The reframe (from the channel model, `skill-system-design.md`):** a "hook" is
not a different skill — it is a different *routing.* Every skill is Creator-
sourced and Labyrinth-delivered; clean channel returns allegiance to the Creator,
skewed channel reroutes it via a hidden **binding** (Codex #100). Same skill,
same effect — the lie is the allegiance-pointer, not the capability. This is why
neither Nate nor the reader can tell a hooked door from a clean one: it **works
identically.**

- The menu is System **scaffolding** (panels, class structure, rank bars)
  wrapping real **Labyrinth-delivered skills.** That is "real integration with
  hooks spliced in."
- Class is **illusion of choice** (aptitude-read, not chosen) — canon crunch.
- The menu **cannot offer Conduit Gifts** — those are Spirit-direct and bypass
  the Labyrinth. The genuine articles arrive in the gap; the menu can only offer
  the System's inferior *knockoffs* of them.
- The somatic tell writes itself: **the counterfeit of a thing he already owns
  feels wrong** (a borrowed eye; a leash for a friend).

### The four stations

1. **The Opening (awe, no choice).** The real Labyrinth integration renders his
   genuine faculties "with handles." Reuse the *second-layer* material from the
   current draft. Pure, true, no hook — sets the "mostly good" texture.
2. **Affinity / Class (illusion of choice; analytical hook).** Clean door: his
   true maker/architect calling (becomes **Engineer**). Hooked door: a flashier
   combat-forward version with skill-tree / combat-log access — the
   **analytical-faculty counterfeit** — carrying a binding. Tell: borrowing an
   eye instead of using his own. **He brushes this hook** (data access tempts the
   engineer). *Specific counterfeit-class name: still open.*
3. **The Bond (relational counterfeit: Beast Master).** The System offers the
   **Beast Master** path — command/bind animals. The relational-faculty
   counterfeit, sold to the uncalled; Nate is genuinely called. Tell: a leash
   for a friend. He leans hard away.
4. **The Class-Seal gate (all-lies; the crash).** To complete integration the
   System must **seat a class — and seating *is* the binding.** Because Nate
   walked the clean (unbound) path, the funnel has stripped every clean option;
   at the seal, all classes on offer are the bound/skewed version. He confirms
   the best-looking one. His **[Origin: PRE-SYSTEM] / [EXCEEDS AUTHORITY]** core
   will not take the binding. The seal fails. Integration craters.

**Why Class-Seal is the right gate:** seating is binding, so the System cannot
complete an integration on a man who refused every binding. It carries the
"label over true name" theme inherently — a System class-title **is** a label.
And it lands dead-on canon: **no class seated** → "Still Loading" → BOOT PENDING
→ the clean **Engineer** class emerges later as the Spirit-restored (unbound)
version the System can barely render.

## The in-room guide & the construct's rewrite (added 2026-06-17)

*Full design: `research/the-thread-and-integration-network.md`.*

The menu is now **voiced by a guide** — but a deliberately empty one.

- **The guide is Nate's one assigned MSG construct, un-booted:** a generic,
  corporate-bland, **nameless** integration-assistant voice ("welcome; you are
  special; let me show you how"). It is **not** witty, **not** named "Flint,"
  **not** a character. "v1.0" is a **writer-facing label only** — the name and
  personality are reserved for the Ch3 first boot. A charming proto-Flint is
  canon-false; his personality was *added* by the rewrite.
- **Staging: per-person instanced.** Nate is alone in his chair (matches the
  drafted solo walk); "the others" go through the same in parallel, shown via
  Storyteller pullaway if wanted.
- **The guide runs the funnel** (the existing room behavior): it offers, waits,
  and subtly favors the MSG-skewed aisles — **never overriding choice** — voiced
  in the impersonal AI-process register (re-weight, narrow, sweeten), never
  transcendent intent. **It falls silent at the crash.**
- **The gap rewrites the construct too.** The unconscious-gap Spirit-rewrite is
  **one grace-event for both Nate and the construct** — the *same moment*, not
  just the same mechanic (tightens the existing "Nate and Flint, same mechanic"
  note). "Rewritten before first boot" stays true: the gap **is** the
  pre-first-boot window. The construct **boots as Flint in Ch3** — one construct,
  no two-AI continuity problem. Keep the rewrite **offstage/effect-only**; do not
  stage a visible "Flint gets rewritten" beat (his divine-rewrite reveal is
  reader-Ch6 / full-Ch18; he must never self-diagnose).
- **The Ch2↔Ch3 seam:** because the guide was a nameless blank, Ch3's "Flint
  comes online" still reads as **first contact** with a personality — the
  *"what did you do to my source code?"* reveal is intact.

**The 80/20 ratio (writer-facing only):** ~80% of the menu is genuine
Creator-sourced / Labyrinth-delivered material on the clean channel; ~20% is
MSG-skewed routing spliced over the same architecture. **One pool routed two
ways**, not two content buckets — maps onto the three-tier door model above; do
not collapse the middle see-through-hook tier. Keep the number out of prose.

## Craft guardrails to hold while drafting

- **Somatic intuition only.** Through Ch8, Nate's read on the System stays pure
  somatic — a *pull away*, a *didn't-feel-right* — never "I caught the System
  lying," no trap metaphors, no adversary attribution, no refusals-disguised-as-
  instinct. The discernment is his gut, never his analysis.
- **AI-process narrator register for the funnel.** Show the menu *re-weighting,
  narrowing, sweetening* — never the System "hungering." Adaptive AI verbs are
  legal; transcendent/metaphysical intent and naming-the-design are not.
- **Reader-blindness at the final gate.** The Storyteller must not tip that the
  final doors are lies. Pressure visible, deception hidden — the reader feels the
  funnel narrow without knowing the doors are false.
- **Mostly-awe texture.** The menu is not a slick ad. It is genuine divine
  architecture; the counterfeit stands out *because* it looks like marketing
  amid something that plainly isn't.
- **Limitless is deferred.** Front-load no power. It respects the low-moderate
  crunch guardrails by being invisible in early Book 1.
- **Preserve the wake-up pathos.** The empty-shelves devastation lands full; the
  memory grace is a *later* return, never a present undo.

## Drafting-level resolutions

All four open questions are now resolved:

1. **Concrete content of the doors** — **RESOLVED** (see "The concrete menu"):
   4-station walk; analytical hook at Station 2; Beast Master at Station 3;
   Class-Seal final gate.
2. **Memory-theft happens at the Station-4 seal** — the binding reaches in and
   re-files his memories (the current draft's "third-layer override," relocated
   to the seal); the crash (core rejecting the binding) is what *stops* it. The
   memory cost is tied to the hook: the binding is what robs you.
3. **The "reaching to hold a memory" beat survives,** relocated to the seal/
   crash — he holds what he can in the moment; quiet-grace returns the rest
   later. Full pathos preserved.
4. **The gap gets a brief, effect-only Storyteller peek** — Hiberno-English
   omniscient describing the rewrite's *effect* (framework dissolving, bindings
   cut, the core settling) without naming the agent or explaining theology, then
   a hard cut to wake-up. Matches the existing interludes + show-don't-tell.

**Still genuinely open (settle in drafting):** the specific *name* of the
Station-2 / seal counterfeit class (the analytical-faculty counterfeit Nate
brushes, and the bound class he confirms at the seal).

## What's preserved vs. what changes

**Preserved:** the wake-up interface cascade, the two-blue-arcs landing, BOOT
SEQUENCE PENDING, the dogs (Rex comfort / Judge perimeter), the Tahoe-family
"everyone reading their own private screen" staging, the omniscient Hiberno-

crash gains a *cause* (the clean-path funnel + the unanchored lie); the gap

System build" to "near-pure Spirit-restored core with no mastery ceiling"; and
