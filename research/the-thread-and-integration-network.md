## The three-layer network stack

The thing that connects Earth to everything else has three layers. Keep them
distinct — most of the early confusion came from collapsing them.

1. **The Labyrinth** *(Codex #17, clean substrate / pipeline)* — God's
   multiverse routing infrastructure. Always clean. The bones under everything.
2. **The Thread** *(NEW)* — a futuristic, multiverse-spanning **information
   network carried on the Labyrinth.** It replaces today's copper and fiber. It
   is the source of most **HUD data.** Clean substrate; readable by every
   companion AI.
3. **The System / MSG overlay** *(Codex #4, #56)* — Morningstar's metering +
   surveillance + corruption layer riding on top of The Thread. This is what
   humans and AIs *experience* as the comms/info layer; it meters traffic,
   surveils it, and shades what it passes through.

**Master principle:** *substrate clean, overlay corrupt.* The Thread is true;
the System is the lens that shades it. Morningstar doesn't own the truth — it
owns the lens.

## Earth's dimensional unfolding (the world gets bigger)

During the **~2-week Tutorial window**, the Labyrinth connection solidifies and
Earth's sealed / folded pocket-space **un-compresses.**

- Traversal scale roughly **triples.** The distance between the original settled
  pieces grows; **continents keep their shapes**; **cities and settlements do
  NOT move or change** — they simply end up farther apart, with new unfolded
  ground opening *between* them.
- The **ranch stays the scale Nate knows.** The new vastness appears in the gaps.
- This is **folded space un-compressing**, not crude planetary inflation — it is
  Veil-break un-sealing (existing canon) applied at scale. This is what
  preserves Codex #30: *"still East Texas; keep enough normalcy that the loss
  hurts."*
- **Permanent.** The unfolded geography is the new stable Earth map. (The
  *aesthetic* Labyrinth-bleed at connection points still recedes over time per
  existing canon — raw scale and aesthetic drift are **two separate clocks.**)
- **Rendered offstage:** the cohort is inside the Tutorial pocket while it
  happens, so it is delivered via Storyteller Camera-pullaways / global
  interludes, aligned with the integration-global-scope reveal (~Ch9).

### The new frontier

- The unfolded spaces are where the Labyrinth's connections are **strongest.**
- They fill with fantasy-world features per **Veil-break + Labyrinth-bleed**: new
  animals, fauna, minerals/metals; world-specific drift is heaviest near
  connection points and **fades to Earth-native toward the old settled cores.**
- Built-in **danger gradient:** strongest connection → wildest ground → richest
  / most-exotic resources → highest threat. Directly feeds Cornerstone / frontier
  stakes.

## The grid die-off (power)

- The old grid breaks because **copper and fiber cannot span the unfolded
  distance** — the lines snap.
- **Fuel-fed power plants keep their immediate area lit while fuel lasts;**
  everywhere else goes dark on a staggered clock.
- **Magic / System utilities replace electricity, and people have to learn to
  adapt.**
- This **reinforces** the seductive-collapse model (Codex #84): replacement, not
  instant destruction. The old world runs down at the speed of its fuel reserves
  while the new layer becomes the only thing that works everywhere.

## The Thread as information utility

- It replaces "the internet." (*"Internet" is writer-facing and banned in prose
  per fantasy register — render it through effect.*)
- It carries the world's information across worlds and is the **source of most
  HUD data.**
- **Every** companion AI reads The Thread. The differentiator is the **lens:**
  standard AIs read it *through the MSG overlay* (metered, surveilled, shaded)
  and **report every query upstream** (spyware, Codex #56).
- **Flint** reads it through his **unmonitored ghost credential, below the MSG
  overlay** — same data, truer, and unseen.

## Flint's access model (refined)

- **"Receive-only / can't call back" is scoped to the MSG / System layer ONLY:**
  Flint cannot transmit or report *out* to Morningstar. (Codex #2, #56 unchanged
  in spirit; scope clarified.)
- On **The Thread**, Flint **reads freely** — lookup is a *receive* operation,
  not throttled by the MSG limit. This is the "look things up like a research
  assistant" capability David wants: email, voicemail, records, general info,
  anything on the wire and powered.
- **Both the Thread read AND the ghost credential PERSIST everywhere** — Earth,
  the Tutorial pocket, and the deep Labyrinth.
  - **This SUPERSEDES** the prior note that Flint's ghost access is *lost when
    crossing into the wider Labyrinth* (REFERENCE.md "Flint Quantum Substrate +
    Ghost Access," 2026-04). It also aligns with `flint.md`'s Network Access
    Progression, where access *deepens* across the series rather than dropping.
  - **Consequence:** deep-Labyrinth tension now comes from **threat, scarcity,
    and terrain** — not from an info-blackout. Flint is no longer the reason
    they're blind out there.
- The 7-book **"Network Access Progression"** in `flint.md` still governs Flint's
  penetration of the **System / MSG network** (root-write by Book 7). That arc is
  about the **corrupt overlay**, NOT The Thread. Keep the two networks distinct.

### Why the HUD undercounts

HUD world-data flows up from the **clean Thread** but passes **through the MSG
overlay**, which shades and meters it on the way to the screen — which is *why*
"Nate's HUD reads LESS while the real effect is MORE" (Codex #75; ch02 design).
Flint, reading below the overlay, sees the truer number. This is the plumbing
under the Book-3 **"Dual Signal"** reveal (`flint.md`) and the
`flint-divine-rewrite` schedule.

## Flint's origin: the nameless construct → Flint

*(Refines E1 + E10 against the locked Ch2 redesign and `flint.md`.)*

- During Ch2 Integration, Nate's **one assigned MSG construct** runs the
  character-creation menu as a **generic, corporate-bland, NAMELESS
  integration-assistant voice** — the un-booted template. Every recipient hears
  the same kind of voice.
- **Staging: per-person instanced.** Nate is alone in his chair (matches the
  drafted solo menu-walk); "the others" go through the same thing in parallel,
  shown via Storyteller pullaway if wanted.
- **"v1.0" is a WRITER-FACING label only.** The name "Flint" and any personality
  **never appear on the page before Ch3.** A charming proto-Flint is canon-false —
  the personality was *added* by the rewrite.
- The guide subtly **favors the MSG-skewed (hooked) options but never overrides
  choice** (the E3 funnel) and **falls silent at the crash.**
- In the **unconscious gap after the crash**, the Spirit rewrites **Nate AND the
  construct in ONE grace-event** — the *same moment*, not merely the same
  mechanic. *"Rewritten before first boot"* stays true: the gap **is** the
  pre-first-boot window.
- The construct **boots in Ch3 as Flint** — the only Flint anyone meets. Ch3's
  *"what did you do to my source code?"* reveal is intact. **One construct, no
  two-AI continuity problem.**

## The 80/20 split (writer-facing)

- A **design ratio only** — not prose, not a stated number (per
  `feedback_prose_numbers_are_atmosphere` / the Narnia Principle).
- ~**80%** of Integration content is genuine **Creator-sourced, Labyrinth-
  DELIVERED** material on the **clean channel**; ~**20%** is **MSG-skewed
  ROUTING** spliced over the same architecture.
- It is **one pool of skills routed two ways** (clean vs hooked binding), **not**
  two buckets of separately-authored content (per `skill-system-design.md`
  channel model; Codex #100). Avoid the label "Labyrinth-original."
- Maps onto the **three-tier door model** (clean / see-through hook / outright
  lie) — do **not** collapse the middle "see-through hook" tier; it is what lets
  Nate discern.

## Naming & prose register

- The network's in-world name: **The Thread.** Ariadne's thread through the
  Labyrinth — the line you follow through the maze is the line that connects the
  worlds and carries the data. Flint's native metaphor: *"let me pull the thread
  on that."*
- The bare word **"Labyrinth" is usable as a place-name** in prose; cosmology
  **labels** (pipeline, substrate, clean channel, node, overlay) stay
  writer-facing. The Integration intro is rendered through **effect** (a
  multiverse opened, the Veil broke), not explained cosmology; the MSG guide may
  give the sanitized brand framing ("welcome," "integration").
