## The Four-Lens Perception Model (Codex #92 — LOCKED)

Nate perceives the world through four lenses. Each fires naturally based on context. No single lens dominates more than 40% of any chapter.

**Lens 1 — RANCH:** Physical survival, terrain, animal behavior, weather, practical labor.
- *Fires when:* Securing resources, reading land, handling animals, physical work
- *Voice:* "Water first. Shelter second. Everything else is a luxury."

**Lens 2 — ENGINEERING:** Infrastructure, architecture, System processes, root cause analysis.
- *Fires when:* Reading System constructs, diagnosing problems, seeing what's load-bearing
- *Voice:* "That's not a dungeon. That's a routing table with a skin on it."

**Lens 3 — GAMER:** Pattern recognition, genre awareness, mechanical thinking.
- *Fires when:* Combat encounters, Tutorial mechanics, recognizing game design patterns
- *Voice:* "Adaptive difficulty. Miniboss with adds. The Tutorial is running an AI director."

**Lens 4 — CHRISTIAN:** Quiet moral compass. Not spoken — acted. The Narnia Principle lens.
- *Fires when:* Decisions with moral weight, moments of sacrifice, costly right over easy wrong
- *Voice:* No voice. It IS the decision. He feeds people before himself. He goes back for the person everyone left behind. He doesn't explain why.

**Combat activation pattern:** Gamer recognizes → Engineer confirms → Rancher executes → Christian decides.

## Tutorial Loadout (Codex #104 — LOCKED)

Nate does NOT pick a weapon from the racks. He picks materials and BUILDS his loadout.

**Primary — Improvised Crossbow (Ranged):** Strips a rack crossbow for its prod (decent spring steel). Builds new stock from a table strut (structural wood). Files trigger from a metal bracket. Bonds with Field Repair. Ugly, barn-built, shoots harder than anything on the racks.

**Ammunition — Thornling Thorn Bolts:** Josie Pickett (Appraiser) collects structural thorns from combat rooms. Nate crafts them into bolts at rest stations. The Tutorial's creatures as ammo against itself.

**Backup — Reinforced Hammer (Melee + Crafting Tool):** Crafting hammer, reinforced with Field Repair. Two roles: backup melee + forge tool at every crafting station.

**No names.** They're tools, not Excalibur.
